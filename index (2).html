Our Story

A Passion for Perfect Cakes

Founded in 2012, Raman Cake began as a small home kitchen with one mission: to create cakes so beautiful and delicious that they'd become the centrepiece of life's most cherished moments.

Today, our artisan bakery has grown into a beloved local institution, serving thousands of families, couples, and corporate clients across the city — but our soul remains the same: every cake is made from scratch, with love, by human hands.
